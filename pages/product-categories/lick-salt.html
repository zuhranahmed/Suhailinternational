Animal's Lick Salt

This is beginning of salt licks story when the horses of Alexander licked the rocks when he was on the way to conquer subcontinent and was staying in the valleys of Himalayas, near river Jhelum. His ailing horses untied to graze, started licking the rocks and became surprisingly well as observed by his soldiers who found a salty taste to the rocks. Himalayan Salt lick is complete nutrition and mineral supplement for animals and is necessary nutrients needed by the animals of all age. This is an important part of their daily feed.
Salt licks become more important in in hard weather and environments when the needed nutrients are less available.
Himalayan Salt licks have phosphorus, sodium, calcium, iron, zinc and other trace elements by nature and are required for bones, muscles and digestive system of animals. Himalayan lick salt lick is made of pure natural pink salt in which no additive is implanted at any stage of production.
